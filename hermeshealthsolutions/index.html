Welcome to Hermes Health Solutions

Here at Hermes Health Solutions, we value you like family, and in our family, everyone is the top customer. Because of that, we make sure to provide the best possible experience for you as we make sure that you are treated for everything that we can find. Or not find, and ask you to come back for a follow-up in three months. Whatever makes you the best possible customer who is a happy member of our family. Did we mention that we're a family here? A nation-wide, for-profit family. Here at Hermes Health Solutions, we are the solution.

COVID Update:

We are now back in person following the COVID-19 Pandemic. If you are feeling unwell, please do not come into any of our offices. You are gross and we don't want to get sick. If you absolutely need to, please wear a mask.

Hermes Health Solutions is the Thesis project for Owen Cook, a Graphic Design student at Portland State University. Focusing on User Interface and User Experience Design, he found a particular interest in Web Accessibility as part of his humanist, user-centered values. While researching, he was struck by the realization that accessible design guidelines made complete sense for a universally effective user experience, both for disabled and abled users. This lead to him developing this project, an anti-design experiment in creating an inaccessible site to highlight how valuable accessible design is for all users, including those who may take it for granted. Why make it a healthcare website in particular? Think about it.
